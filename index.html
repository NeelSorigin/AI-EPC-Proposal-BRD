§ 1Overview
Executive Summary
This BRD defines the functional and operational requirements for the Sorigin EPC Automated Proposal platform — an AI-augmented system connecting the complete commercial workflow from lead capture to client-ready techno-commercial proposal.
Business Objective: Enable any Sales Account Manager to generate a complete, accurate SRESPL-format Solar EPC proposal — including site details, GIS layout, project configuration, commercial terms, and OMS pricing — in under four hours, without requiring specialist intervention for standard project types.
Scope
✓ In Scope
  • Solar EPC (ground mount & rooftop)
  • BESS (standard sizing ranges)
  • Maharashtra locations (Solapur, Karmala, Vairagam)
  • AI SLD Generator integration (Bedrock + ODA)
  • NetSuite proposal record creation
  • SRESPL proposal format (PDF output)
✕ Out of Scope
  • Wind turbine projects (pending clarity)
  • Complex / non-standard BESS (specialist-handled)
  • International projects
  • AMS / OMS live monitoring (separate system)
  • NetSuite financial accounting
  • Client e-signature workflow

§ 2Master Workflow
Master Workflow — Lead to First Proposal
Every department connected in a single automated pipeline. Humans approve at gates; AI handles generation. Each row is one department's contribution. Orange gates = mandatory human confirmation before next department begins.
Stage 1
Sales Team
Lead Captured → Capacity Sized — Lead logged from Apollo/LinkedIn or inbound. Bills uploaded; AI extracts kWh, kVA, tariff. Existing renewables deducted. DC/AC MWp, DC:AC ratio, CAPEX/OPEX comparison computed. Location intelligence auto-fetches GHI, nearest substation, voltage, aerial distance.
Gate 1
Execution
Capacity & Site Confirmation — Execution reviews capacity brief and land feasibility. Land area, access road, grid proximity confirmed. Green light: confirmed brief auto-sent to Design team; NetSuite updated to Design In Progress.
Stage 2
Design & Eng.
GIS → SLD → Electrical Design — Satellite image fetched; plot boundary traced; panel rows placed with shading analysis. String config, ACJB, inverter positions, HT yard, evacuation route generated. Engineer reviews → AI revises (max 3 cycles) → senior stamps → DWG/PDF exported via ODA.
Gate 2
Execution
Design Confirmation — Execution reviews panel layout, evacuation route, civil scope, sub-project split. On approval: design package auto-released to SCM; NetSuite status → BOQ Pricing In Progress.
Stage 3
SCM
BOQ Extraction & Pricing — AI generates BOQ from design data. Thumb-rule ₹/MWp × capacity = EPC cost. OMS terms auto-structured: ₹5L/MWp/year, 5% escalation, quarterly advance. Cost-loaded BOQ released to BD.
Stage 4
BD Team
Proposal Assembly & Dispatch — All 10 SRESPL sections auto-populated from NetSuite. BD reviews & edits. Manager approves (historical comps surfaced, AI ₹/MWp recommendation). Sorigin-branded PDF generated. Proposal No. SRESPL/YY/S/[State]/[Seq] assigned. Sent to client.

§ 5Gate
Execution Gate 1 — Capacity & Site Confirmation
Mandatory checkpoint before any design work begins. Execution team reviews the capacity brief and confirms site feasibility.
Check ItemDetail
Capacity appropriatenessIs the MWp achievable on available land? Does DC:AC ratio match site conditions?
Land profile confirmationSite flat/polygon? Access road available? Distance from main road manageable for construction logistics?
Grid connectivity feasibilityIs substation connectivity achievable within cost assumptions? ROW availability for transmission line?
Scope clarificationExecution team can flag capacity splits, sub-project boundaries, or revisions needed.
Gate 1 Approved: Capacity brief + site data auto-sent to Design & Engineering. NetSuite project status → "Design In Progress".
Revision Required: Platform routes back to Sales with specific change request. Revised brief must be resubmitted for Gate 1 re-review before design begins.

§ 10.6Access Control
Role-Based Access Control
JWT-based RBAC enforced across all modules. Every user is assigned exactly one role. Unauthorised access returns HTTP 403.
Viewer
Execution Team (Gate reviewers, read-only) · Senior Engineers reviewing but not editing
Read-only access to proposals, design packages, capacity briefs, and BOQ data. Cannot create, edit, or approve anything.
Editor
Sales AMs · Design Engineers · SCM Team Members
Create and edit records within their assigned department. Upload files, run AI jobs, save design sessions, submit for approval. Cannot approve gate decisions.
Manager
BD Manager · Execution Team Lead · Design Lead · SCM Head
All Editor permissions + approve/revise Gate 1 & Gate 2; approve proposal pricing; stamp and release design packages; confirm pricing KB rates.
Sponsor
CEO · Heads of BD / D&E / SCM · Platform Admin (Sorigin Digital)
Full platform access. Manages user accounts, role assignments, platform configuration. Views all audit logs, proposal registries, AI job histories.
